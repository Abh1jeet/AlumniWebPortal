Message from Dean

Today is an era of technocracy that is boosting forward and the country is progressing implausibly in every sphere. To keep up with this rhythm of fast changing society and producing a remarkable quality of youth is the need of the hour. The increasing numbers of technological institutions keep emerging in the scenario to realize the vision of enlisting India among the developed nations.

G.B Pant University of Agriculture & Technology ranks among the top universities and has set standards of distinction and success because of the alumni of this college.

Our core mission is to engage all alumni for the lifetime. Irrespective of the year of graduation, the Alumni Relations Office is committed towards building and reaffirming the ties connecting the 10000+ members of GBPUAT University alumni family with each other and their Alma mater.

In our endeavor to bring the University on the world map, your role is very vital. Your contributions in the past have resulted in enhancing the image of the University to this level. Your continued support and guidance will certainly help take this University to a much higher level. Big deeds begin with small acts! I invite you all to connect with us so that we can stay engaged with each other as a family.

The college has witnessed growth and augmentation in every field .The graph has been on the upsurge since the day the foundation of this college was laid. 

It is the cooperative venture of faculty and the students and the alumni that today this college stands as an insignia of feat and the same will take it to being an icon in tomorrow’s vista.

Dr. H.C. Sharma
Dean,
College Of Technology
