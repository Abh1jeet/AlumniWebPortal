Message from Vice Chancellor

I am delighted to hear that College of Technology, a constituent faculty of G. B. Pant University of Agriculture and Technology, is organizing SAMANVAY - The Annual Alumni Meet on 26th and 27th November 2016. It has been a unique experiment to establish the College of Technology in an Agriculture University and when we look back at the past, one can safely comment that the experiment has been successful and was later emulated by many other agriculture universities. The college has not only been restricted to core area of

technology i. e. challenging area of Electronics, Computer, IT etc. but has also expanded its domain in other areas of Agriculture, Agri-informatics etc.

The college has worked vigorously on giving its budding talents a new path and attitude in order to make them capable of facing the real world challenges. Hopefully this magazine proves to create a dynamic bond between the college and the alumni.

The Dean of the faculty along with the staff members, students, organizers and alumni deserve my heartiest congratulation for taking this wonderful initiative. I wish that this annual alumni meet SAMANVAY 2016 brings a new era of joy and enlightenment.

Dr. J. Kumar
Vice Chancellor,
G.B. Pant University of Agri. & Tech.
