College Website Team


The college website (www.gbpuat-tech.ac.in) epitomizes the college to the outside world. The College of Technology has its proficient, artistic and enthusiastic team that plays a climacteric role in maintaining the college website. It keeps the people well acquainted with the college and its ongoing activities. It provides contemporary information about the college including the college administration, placement, faculty profiles, course details, directory listings, latest college events, academics and much more. The college website not only helps the students but also strengthens the bond the alumni share with their alma mater.

The college website team also provides information regarding the undertakings of the training and placement cell. It also contains an announcement section which provides information to the students about fee details, information brochure, academic calendar, various conferences beneficial workshops ,training and much more to be held in the college, .It also includes various links ,webmail, discussion forum,photogallery,publications ,campus map and information regarding the numerous events going on in the college throughout the year.

The college website houses the links for the biannual e-magazine:- Audient, the bi-annual newsletter:-Latitude 29 and the college magazine Techniche. Samadhan is the online complaint register where one is free to complain about the various issues and concerns related to the college.

The college website acts as a prospect for the students, the faculty and the alumni to keep them updated with the college and its happenings.
